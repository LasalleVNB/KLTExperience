ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm an illustrator who mainly creates art for thumbnails and music videos, and draws fanworks in their spare time. My activities outside of illustration include singing and occasional dabbles in video editing. My works can vary in style and atmosphere depending on the needs of the client or the piece—I like to think I'm quite versatile! If you need a thumbnail or some sprites, consider me your guy.

CLIENT WORK

My client work mainly consists of thumbnails for cover artists on YouTube and sprites to be used for music videos. I can imitate the styles of other illustrators if need be, or workshop a unique concept to bring the client’s vision to life.
